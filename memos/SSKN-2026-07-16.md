## HEADLINE: THESIS BROKEN — DOWNGRADE TO PARK

The prior 7/12 QUEUED thesis is **invalidated by events**. Since the last scoring pass, SSKN has:
1. Been **Nasdaq-delisted effective February 19, 2026** — failed to cure the $2.5M minimum-stockholders'-equity deficiency
2. Filed Form 25/Form 15 to **go dark** — terminating SEC reporting entirely to cut G&A
3. Had the delisting **trigger an event of default** on its MidCap term loan, reclassifying all long-term debt to current
4. Received a **going-concern qualification** in its FY2025 10-K
5. Seen the equity collapse from ~$0.82/share to **~$0.09** (~−89%)

Altman Z-score −11.59 (deep distress zone). Accumulated deficit $254.4M; FY2025 net loss ~$6.3M.

The cruel irony: **the razor-blade engine itself is fine.** 842 U.S. partner clinics on the per-treatment recurring model; gross billings per device **$5,981 in Q3'25, +8.5% YoY — highest since Q4 2022**; Medicare CPT reimbursement $137–183/treatment. This is a good consumable business strapped to a broken balance sheet that is now being taken out of public-market view. **The equity already re-rated — downward.** What remains is a distressed-debt/recap situation, not an asymmetric public-equity long.

The only realistic value paths (recap, debt-for-equity, distressed sale, or a capital raise off the confidential S-1) are structurally dilutive or wiping to current equity holders.

**C — Confidence / Data Quality: 2/5 and falling**
The FY2025 10-K is solid and current — today's *diagnosis* is high-confidence. But the company is terminating SEC reporting — there will be no Q1 2026 10-Q, no forward audited financials, no reliable go-forward data. Confidence in any future assessment collapses by design.
