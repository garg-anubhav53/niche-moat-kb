## Business Summary

Eiken Chemical Co., Ltd. (TSE: 4549.T) is a Japanese manufacturer of clinical diagnostics, headquartered in Tokyo. Founded 1939. Core products:

1. **OC-SENSOR FIT Reagents** — fecal immunochemical test (FIT) for colorectal cancer screening; used in Japan's national annual health checkup program (MHLW mandate) and in 45+ countries internationally; recognized by US USPSTF as having best-in-class test sensitivity/specificity among FIT platforms.
2. **LAMP Diagnostics** — loop-mediated isothermal amplification (Eiken invented LAMP 1998–2000); Simprova automated LAMP platform; applications in food safety, livestock, infectious disease, and oncology biomarker testing. JLAC + MHLW certified.
3. **Microbiology reagents** — culture media, rapid identification kits.
4. **Immunological/serological reagents** — immunoassay and serology diagnostics.
5. **Urinalysis test strips** — occult blood, protein, glucose.

No US ADR; no English-language analyst coverage from Western sell-side banks.

## §5a — Sub-Assessment Scores

### Q — Business Quality: **3/5**

**Evidence for:**

- OC-SENSOR used in 45+ countries; internationally recognized by USPSTF as best-in-class

**Evidence against:**
- Revenue growth only 3.4% — this is a slow-growth franchise, not an expanding compounder
- LAMP core patents expired (invented 1998–2000; 20-year life = core expiry ~2018–2022); multiple competitors now offer LAMP technology; Eiken's LAMP edge has weakened vs. the moat period
- Overseas OC-SENSOR revenue contribution unknown (no English segment disclosure)
- No English-language disclosure on ROIC, ROE, FCF decomposition
- Net margin 8.8% is decent but not indicative of franchise-level pricing power — Japanese MHLW biennial price revisions cap upside

**Score rationale:** Solid, defensible business with mandate-backed consumable demand. Not a franchise compounder with expanding ROIC or reinvestment runway. A steady-state, capital-light diagnostics manufacturer with government backstop. Q = **3/5**.
