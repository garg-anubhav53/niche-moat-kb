## Moat Summary

**Type:** REGULATORY MONOPOLY (sole FDA+EMA approved blue-light cystoscopy agent)
- Hexvix/Cysview (hexaminolevulinate, HAL) — sole drug-device combination approved by both FDA (Cysview, 2010) and EMA (Hexvix, 2004) for photodiagnosis in bladder cancer detection
- No competing HAL agent has achieved FDA/EMA approval
- Patent protection to at least December 2036 (US Patent 10,556,010)
- Installed-base: 390+ rigid blue-light cystoscopy (BLC) systems in US hospitals
- Clinical evidence base is 20+ years deep — substitution requires full clinical validation (prohibitive)
- **Revenue model:** royalty/milestone from Ipsen (EU), license from Karl Storz (device), commercialization via own US entity (Photocure Inc.)
